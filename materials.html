Materials By Brand

Can’t decide between Copper, Weathered Steel or Titanium Zinc? Tossing up between Monument®, Baslat® and Surfmist®? We are here to support you in those decisions.

All of our cladding, roofing, flashings and custom project designs are fabricated in our factory in Bacchus Marsh. We have a large range of alloys for you to choose from to achieve the desired result on your project.

We have worked with the best construction companies in Victoria and New South Wales for the past 12 years which has allowed us to work with all metal types. We have extensive experience with what metal is suited for what application and outcome.
