Signs that your roof may need replacement include missing or broken shingles, leaks, water damage, visible sagging, and age. If your roof is over 20 years old, it may be time for an inspection to determine if replacement is necessary.

The cost of a new roof depends on several factors such as the size of the roof, materials used, labor costs, and the complexity of the project. Contact us for a free estimate based on your specific needs.

The time required for a roof installation or replacement varies depending on the size and type of roof. On average, a residential roof replacement can take 1-3 days. Larger or more complex projects may take longer.
